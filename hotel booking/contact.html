Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

You can modify or cancel your reservation through our website or mobile app. Log in to your account, go to "My Bookings," and follow the prompts. Most reservations can be cancelled for free up to 48 hours before check-in.

If you find a lower price for the same room, on the same dates, at the same hotel on another website within 24 hours of booking, we'll match that price and give you an additional 10% discount. Contact our customer support with proof of the lower rate.

Signing up is free and easy! Simply create an account on our website or mobile app, and you'll automatically be enrolled in our LuxStay Rewards program. You'll start earning points immediately on all eligible bookings.

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. We also accept payments through PayPal and Apple Pay. Some hotels may accept additional payment methods at check-in.
