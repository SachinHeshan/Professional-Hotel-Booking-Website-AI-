Our Story

Discover the journey behind LuxStay and our commitment to redefining luxury travel experiences

Who We Are

LuxStay was born from a simple idea: travel should be effortless, luxurious, and memorable. Founded in 2010 by travel enthusiasts, we've grown from a small startup to a global leader in luxury hotel bookings.

Today, we connect discerning travelers with exceptional accommodations in over 120 countries, offering unparalleled service, exclusive deals, and a seamless booking experience.

2010

The Beginning

Founded in a small apartment with a vision to transform luxury travel. Our first booking was a boutique hotel in Bali.

2014

Global Expansion

Expanded to 30 countries and launched our award-winning mobile app, making luxury travel accessible anywhere.

2018

Sustainability Initiative

Launched our Green Stay program, partnering with eco-friendly hotels and promoting sustainable tourism.

2023

Today & Beyond

Serving over 1 million travelers annually with a curated collection of 15,000+ luxury properties worldwide.
